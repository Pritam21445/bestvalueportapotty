Frequently Asked Questions

Why is there a urinal in the standard porta potty?

It’s a practical design feature to help keep the main toilet seat cleaner and to extend the service life of the unit by reducing the use of toilet paper.

What do you do differently for winter rentals?

We use a special anti-freeze brine in the waste tank to prevent it from freezing solid. We also require our customers to provide a clear, safe path for our delivery and service trucks in snowy conditions.

What's the most important thing for a fall festival?

Besides having enough units for the crowd, a large, multi-bay handwashing station is the most critical component for a family-friendly fall event.

What is the cost of a porta potty for a weekend?

The cost for a porta potty for a weekend is very affordable. The exact porta potty rental price depends on your location and the type of unit you choose. Contact us for a quick, practical quote.

Can I put a heater inside a standard porta potty?

No. For safety reasons, you should never place any type of external heating element inside a standard plastic porta potty. If you need heat, our fully-equipped luxury restroom trailers are the safe and correct solution.
